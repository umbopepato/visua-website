Motivation

Once you have created your company's identity you want to iterate those concepts to all of your products (being them websites, apps, marketing material, document templates...) but there is a moment in which that data is translated manually by developers, marketers and designers. Visua wants to remove that manual intervention by letting you code that information into a set of files and use that for your builds
